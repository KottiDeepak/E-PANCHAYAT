About us...

The E-panchayat website is a digital platform designed to enhance the efficiency and transparency of Panchayati Raj institutions in India. It provides various services and information related to rural development, governance, and citizen services. This website reducing paperwork and corruption. It helps rural communicaties by providing quick services and real time informations.
